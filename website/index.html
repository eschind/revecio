01 The opportunity

Trillions of dollars sit behind the institutions that hold society together — and most of them are underserved.

Foundations, endowments, and nonprofits steward more than $2 trillion in assets in the United States alone. The returns on that capital sustain hospitals, universities, scientific research, and the social safety net.

Yet the institutions managing this capital face a tightening squeeze: persistent inflation, declining government support, growing programmatic demand, and increasingly complex markets — all while operating with lean teams and tools designed for a different era.

  • $2T+ U.S. foundation, endowment & nonprofit assets
  • 5% minimum annual payout most foundations must meet
  • 1 in 3 institutions report rising operational pressure
02 Our approach

A single intelligent system — not a stack of tools stitched together.

The traditional OCIO is a service business with software bolted on. Reve inverts that. We start with a unified, agentic AI system that reasons across the full surface of an institution's portfolio: markets, managers, liquidity, policy, operations, and reporting.

One system, one source of truth, working continuously — augmenting the judgment of investment teams rather than replacing it, and making institutional-grade investing accessible to organizations that have historically been priced out of it.
